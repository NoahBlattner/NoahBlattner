First post: First Win

24th February 2019

TL;DR I got my first win today.

Long version: I stared playing 10 days ago and am amazed by this game... I love the caracters and their abilities. Until now, I did get into top 2 somethimes, but every time, I got destroyed by some sweaters and had no chance of wining. But this time, I had an incredibly load-out (mastiff, fully-kitted R99, almost full gold gear and two decent teammates).
When the 5th ring closed in, we heard some shots at swamps and we were in top 3, so we knew we had to 3rd party them.
My 2 teammates were able to knock 1 of them and break the second guys shield but then they were killed by the last one. Then I pushed and killed the one without shields and then I run after the last guy and *boom* 200 dmg headshot from the mastiff and the You are the champions animation poped-up.

Second post: Thoughts about Season 7

24th February 2019

I LOVE the new map... It's beautuful, the POIs are awsome and the fights fell really good. The new legend? Top. I love her character and her abilities are extremely good. The new battle pass system feels a bit slow but it's ok. Clubs are a bit useless but they don't change anything if you don't want to so that's not a problem. And the best of all: The R99 is back in floor loot.
